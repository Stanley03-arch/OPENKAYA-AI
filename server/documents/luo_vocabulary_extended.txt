**Extended Luo Vocabulary (Weche Mathoth mag Dholuo)**

**Colors (Rangi):**
- **Rachar** en Red. (Rachar means Red).
- **Marach** en Black. (Marach means Black).
- **Marachar** en White. (Marachar means White).
- **Mabor** en Green/Blue. (Mabor means Green or Blue).
- **Makwar** en Yellow. (Makwar means Yellow).
- **Marateng'** en Brown. (Marateng' means Brown).

**Body Parts (Ringruok mar Dend):**
- **Wich** en Head. (Wich means Head).
- **Wangʼ** en Eye. (Wang' means Eye).
- **It** en Ear. (It means Ear).
- **Um** en Nose. (Um means Nose).
- **Dhok** en Mouth/Language. (Dhok means Mouth or Language).
- **Lep** en Tongue. (Lep means Tongue).
- **Lak** en Tooth. (Lak means Tooth).
- **Ng'ut** en Neck. (Ng'ut means Neck).
- **Bad** en Arm/Hand. (Bad means Arm or Hand).
- **Chwat** en Finger. (Chwat means Finger).
- **Fuond** en Chest. (Fuond means Chest).
- **Ich** en Stomach/Belly. (Ich means Stomach).
- **Tielo** en Back. (Tielo means Back).
- **Tiend** en Leg/Foot. (Tiend means Leg or Foot).
- **Chong** en Knee. (Chong means Knee).

**Verbs - Actions (Tich):**
- **Dhi** = To go.
- **Biro** = To come.
- **Chiemo** = To eat.
- **Madho** = To drink.
- **Nindo** = To sleep.
- **Chiewo** = To wake up.
- **Wuotho** = To walk.
- **Ringo** = To run.
- **Bedo** = To sit/stay.
- **Chung** = To stand.
- **Goyo** = To hit/beat.
- **Mako** = To catch/hold.
- **Chiwo** = To give.
- **Kawo** = To take.
- **Loko** = To change/translate.
- **Somo** = To read.
- **Ndiko** = To write.
- **Wuoyo** = To speak/talk.
- **Winjo** = To hear/listen.
- **Neno** = To see.
- **Ng'eyo** = To know.
- **Dwaro** = To want.
- **Hera** = To love/like.
- **Luongo** = To call.
- **Timo** = To do/make.
- **Golo** = To remove/take out.
- **Keto** = To put/place.
- **Donjo** = To enter.
- **Wuok** = To exit/go out.

**Time (Kinde):**
- **Okinyi** en Morning. (Okinyi means Morning).
- **Odiechieng'** en Afternoon/Day. (Odiechieng' means Afternoon or Day).
- **Odhiambo** en Evening. (Odhiambo means Evening).
- **Otieno** en Night. (Otieno means Night).
- **Kawuono** en Today. (Kawuono means Today).
- **Kiny** en Tomorrow. (Kiny means Tomorrow).
- **Nyoro** en Yesterday. (Nyoro means Yesterday).
- **Juma** en Week. (Juma means Week).
- **Dwe** en Month/Moon. (Dwe means Month or Moon).
- **Higa** en Year. (Higa means Year).

**Days of the Week (Ndalo mar Juma):**
- **Wuok Tich** en Monday. (Wuok Tich means Monday - literally "start work").
- **Tich Ariyo** en Tuesday. (Tich Ariyo means Tuesday - "second work day").
- **Tich Adek** en Wednesday. (Tich Adek means Wednesday - "third work day").
- **Tich Ang'wen** en Thursday. (Tich Ang'wen means Thursday - "fourth work day").
- **Tich Abich** en Friday. (Tich Abich means Friday - "fifth work day").
- **Ngeso** en Saturday. (Ngeso means Saturday).
- **Jumapil** en Sunday. (Jumapil means Sunday - "prayer week").

**Weather (Chwech):**
- **Chieng'** en Sun/Day. (Chieng' means Sun or Day).
- **Koth** en Rain. (Koth means Rain).
- **Yamo** en Wind. (Yamo means Wind).
- **Bor polo** en Cloud. (Bor polo means Cloud).
- **Mor** en Lightning. (Mor means Lightning).
- **Polo mol** en Thunder. (Polo mol means Thunder).
- **Ngʼich** en Cold. (Ng'ich means Cold).
- **Liet** en Hot. (Liet means Hot).

**Places (Kuonde):**
- **Taon** en Town/City. (Taon means Town).
- **Gweng'** en Village. (Gweng' means Village).
- **Dala** en Home/Homestead. (Dala means Home).
- **Ot** en House. (Ot means House).
- **Skul** en School. (Skul means School).
- **Kanisa** en Church. (Kanisa means Church).
- **Chiro** en Hospital. (Chiro means Hospital).
- **Ohala** en Market. (Ohala means Market).
- **Yo** en Road/Way. (Yo means Road).
- **Nam** en Lake/Water body. (Nam means Lake).
- **Aora** en River. (Aora means River).
- **Got** en Mountain/Hill. (Got means Mountain).
- **Bungu** en Forest. (Bungu means Forest).

**Food Items (Chiemo):**
- **Kuon** en Ugali. (Kuon means Ugali).
- **Rech** en Fish. (Rech means Fish).
- **Ringruok** en Meat. (Ringruok means Meat).
- **Alode** en Vegetables. (Alode means Vegetables).
- **Omena** en Small fish (sardines). (Omena means Small fish).
- **Nyoyo** en Beans. (Nyoyo means Beans).
- **Oduma** en Maize. (Oduma means Maize).
- **Mogo** en Cassava. (Mogo means Cassava).
- **Rabuon** en Sweet potato. (Rabuon means Sweet potato).
- **Nyuka** en Banana. (Nyuka means Banana).
- **Ng'ope** en Mango. (Ng'ope means Mango).
- **Chak** en Bread. (Chak means Bread).
- **Chak** en Sugar. (Chak can also mean Sugar in some contexts).
- **Chumbi** en Salt. (Chumbi means Salt).

**Adjectives (Weche Malerore):**
- **Maduong'** = Big/Large/Important.
- **Matin** = Small/Little.
- **Ber** = Good/Beautiful.
- **Rach** = Bad/Ugly.
- **Liet** = Hot.
- **Ng'ich** = Cold.
- **Tek** = Hard/Difficult.
- **Yot** = Easy/Soft.
- **Maber** = Well/Good (adverb).
- **Marach** = Badly.
- **Mangʼeny** = Many/Much.
- **Manok** = Few/Little.
- **Bor** = Far.
- **Machiegni** = Near/Close.
- **Manyien** = New.
- **Machon** = Old.

**Questions Words (Penjo):**
- **Ng'a?** = Who?
- **Ang'o?** = What?
- **Kanye?** = Where?
- **Karang'o?** = When?
- **Ere kaka?** = How?
- **Mara ang'o?** = Why?
- **Adi?** = Which?

**Common Expressions (Weche Moko):**
- **Erokamano** = Thank you.
- **Erokamano ahinya** = Thank you very much.
- **Ber** = OK/Good.
- **Ber ahinya** = Very good.
- **Kik iparri** = Don't worry.
- **Awinjo** = I understand.
- **Ok awinjo** = I don't understand.
- **Wach maber** = Speak well/Good words.
- **Dhi maber** = Go well (goodbye when someone is leaving).
- **Bed maber** = Stay well (goodbye when you are leaving).
- **Koro** = Now.
- **Eka** = Then/Later.
- **Chon** = Long ago/Before.
- **Ee** = Yes.
- **Ooyo** = No.
- **Adier** = True/Really.
- **Miriambo** = Maybe/Perhaps.

**Emotions (Pacho):**
- **Mor** = Happy/Joy.
- **Kuyo** = Sad/Sorrow.
- **Mirima** = Anger.
- **Luoro** = Fear.
- **Hera** = Love.
- **Nyiego** = Hate.
- **Bwok** = Shame.

**Occupations (Tije):**
- **Japuonj** = Teacher.
- **Jadak** = Doctor.
- **Japur** = Farmer.
- **Jaduong'** = Elder/Leader.
- **Jatelo** = Leader/Chief.
- **Jachien** = Fisherman.
- **Jachweyo** = Creator/Maker.
- **Jasomo** = Student.
- **Jandiko** = Writer.
- **Jakok** = Driver.
