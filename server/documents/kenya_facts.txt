The President of Kenya (Rais wa Kenya, Rais mar Kenya) is **William Ruto**. He was elected in 2022.
Kenya is a country in East Africa with coastline on the Indian Ocean (Nam Lolwe / Bahari ya Hindi).
**Nairobi** is the capital city (Mji mkuu) of Kenya.
**Mombasa** is a coastal city in Kenya.
**Kisumu** is a city on Lake Victoria (Nam Lolwe).
**Luo** (Dholuo) is a Nilotic language spoken in western Kenya.
**Kikuyu** (Gikuyu) is a Bantu language spoken in central Kenya.
Swahili and English are the official languages of Kenya.
**Masai Mara** is a famous game reserve in Kenya.
**Mount Kenya** (Kirinyaga) is the highest mountain in Kenya.
**Population** of Kenya is approximately 55 million people.
**Currency** of Kenya is the Kenyan Shilling (Shilingi).
