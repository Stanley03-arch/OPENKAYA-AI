## Practical Luo Dialogues
Dialogue 1:
- A: Misawa, inyalo konya gi assignment?
- B: Ee, anyalo. Chiw penjo ma idwaro dwoko.

Dialogue 2:
- A: Ok awinjo maber.
- B: Ber. Wabiro dwoko kendo e yore ma yot.

Dialogue 3:
- A: Adwaro yudo tich mar teknologi.
- B: Chak gi basics, tim projects, kendo ket portfolio.

Dialogue 4:
- A: Inyalo loko wechegi e Swahili?
- B: Ee, anyalo loko e Swahili kod English.

## Sentence Construction Patterns
- An + verb + object
  - An adwaro somo.
  - An anyalo timo tich.
- In + verb + object
  - In inyalo penjo gimoro.
- Ka + condition + to + result
  - Ka ok iwinjo, to penj kendo.
- Mondo + purpose
  - Som maber mondo iwinj.

## School and Exam Content
- Tem = exam
- Somo = studying
- Rieko = understanding
- Dwoko = answer
- Penjo = question
- Buk = book
- Tich mar dom = homework
- Piny mar puonj = learning environment

Q: Atim nade mondo adong' e tem?
A: Pangwa saa mar somo, tim revision, kendo penjo ma ok iwinjo.

Q: Inyalo chweyo revision guide?
A: Ee, anyalo yiko topics, key points, kod practice questions.

## Career and Work Content
- Tich = work
- Yudo tich = get a job
- Chwiny mar tich = work attitude
- Konyruok = teamwork
- Balo saa = time waste
- Timo yot = improve workflow

Q: Atim nade mondo ayud tich?
A: Ket CV maber, tim projects, som skills manyien, kendo timo network.

Q: Inyalo yika cover letter?
A: Ee, chiwa role ma idwaro, to anyiso modeli.

## Business and Money Content
- Biashara = business
- Pesa = money
- Chenro = save
- Chudo = pay
- Nengo = price
- Gweng' = market

Q: Atim nade mondo biashara odhi maber?
A: Ng'eyi jachamo, ket nengo ma ber, kendo ti gi customer service maber.

Q: Inyalo yika business plan?
A: Ee, anyalo yiko objective, market, cost, revenue, kendo risk.

## Technology and AI Content
- Komputer = computer
- Intanet = internet
- Website = website
- Aplikeshon = app
- Data = data
- AI = rieko ma nyiso

Q: AI en ang'o?
A: AI en rieko mar nyiso ma konyo machine ni onyalo ng'eyo, puonjore, kendo dwoko penjo.

Q: Inyalo yika website code?
A: Ee, anyalo chweyo HTML, CSS, JavaScript kod responsive layout.
