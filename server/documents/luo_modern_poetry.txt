**Luo Songs and Poetry (Wende gi Sigendni mag Jaluo):**

**Traditional Song 1: Dunia Mbaya (The World is Tough)**

*Chorus:*
**Dunia mbaya, dunia mbaya**
**Ng'ima tek, ng'ima tek**
**To wan Jaluo, wan motegno**
**Wabiro locho, wabiro locho**

(The world is tough, the world is tough
Life is hard, life is hard
But we Luo, we are strong
We will succeed, we will succeed)

*Verse:*
**Tich matek, to ok wabed gi luoro**
**Wadhi nyime, wadhi nyime**
**Jaluo ok jogi, Jaluo ok jogi**
**Wan ji motegno, wan ji motegno**

(Hard work, but we are not afraid
We move forward, we move forward
Luo don't give up, Luo don't give up
We are strong people, we are strong people)

**Traditional Song 2: Nam Lolwe (Lake Victoria)**

**Nam Lolwe, Nam Lolwe**
**Nam maduong' mar Jaluo**
**Rech mathoth, rech mathoth**
**Chiemo mar Jaluo**

(Lake Victoria, Lake Victoria
The great lake of the Luo
Many fish, many fish
Food of the Luo)

**Jachien dhi e nam okinyi**
**Mako rech ne joode**
**Kuon gi rech, kuon gi rech**
**Chiemo mamit mar Jaluo**

(The fisherman goes to the lake in the morning
Catches fish for his family
Ugali and fish, ugali and fish
The delicious food of the Luo)

**Poetry 1: Dala (Home)**

**Dala en kama chuny yweyo**
**Kama iyudo kwe**
**Kama joodi rito**
**Kama hera nitie**

(Home is where the heart rests
Where you find peace
Where your family protects you
Where love exists)

**Bed mabor nade**
**Chuny pod dwaro dala**
**Nikech dala en dala**
**Onge kama chalo gi dala**

(Be as far as you want
The heart still wants home
Because home is home
There's no place like home)

---

**Poetry 2: Jaluo (The Luo Person)**

**Jaluo en ng'at motegno**
**Ma ok dew chandruok**
**En ng'at ma nigi rieko**
**Kendo ma luoro Nyasaye**

(A Luo is a strong person
Who doesn't fear trouble
A person who has wisdom
And who respects God)

**Jaluo hera osiepene**
**Kendo rito joode**
**En ng'at ma nigi luor**
**Ne jodongo kod timbe machon**

(A Luo loves his friends
And takes care of his family
A person who has respect
For elders and old traditions)

---

**Poetry 3: Ng'ima (Life)**

**Ng'ima chalo gi wuoth**
**Ma ok ing'eyo giko mare**
**Kinde moko ber**
**Kinde moko rach**

(Life is like a journey
Whose end you don't know
Sometimes it's good
Sometimes it's bad)

**To nyaka ibed motegno**
**Kendo idhi nyime**
**Nikech ng'ima ok rit ng'ato**
**Kendo chieng' biro wuok**

(But you must be strong
And move forward
Because life doesn't wait for anyone
And the sun will rise)

---

**Poetry 4: Hera (Love)**

**Hera en gimoro maduong'**
**Ma miyo chuny mor**
**En gimoro ma ok nyal ng'iewo**
**To inyalo miyo**

(Love is something great
That makes the heart happy
It's something you cannot buy
But you can give)

**Hera adier ok lal**
**Osiko nyaka giko**
**En gimoro ma duong'**
**Moloyo mwandu duto**

(True love doesn't fade
It lasts until the end
It's something valuable
More than all wealth)

---

**Poetry 5: Rieko (Wisdom)**

**Rieko ok dongo gi higa**
**To gi puonj kod neno**
**Ng'at mariek winjo wach**
**Kendo opuodho kuom kethone**

(Wisdom doesn't grow with age
But with teaching and seeing
A wise person listens to advice
And learns from his mistakes)

**Rieko en mwandu maduong'**
**Moloyo pesa kod dhahabu**
**Ng'at ma nigi rieko**
**Rito ngimane maber**

(Wisdom is great wealth
More than money and gold
A person who has wisdom
Guards his life well)

**Common Luo Sayings in Modern Context:**

1. **"Simu ok chiemo"** (The phone doesn't eat)
   - Meaning: Don't spend all your time on the phone, neglecting real life
   - Usage: Wuoro wacho ni "Simu ok chiemo, bi ichiemo!"

2. **"Online ok dala"** (Online is not home)
   - Meaning: Virtual life is not real life
   - Usage: Kik ibed online pile, bi idhi dala.

3. **"Data ok ng'iewo osiep"** (Data doesn't buy friends)
   - Meaning: Real relationships matter more than online connections
   - Usage: In nigi followers mathoth to ok in gi osiep adier.

4. **"Selfie ok nyiso ng'ima adier"** (Selfies don't show real life)
   - Meaning: Social media doesn't reflect reality
   - Usage: Kik ipar ni ng'ima mar ji ber nikech selfie maber.

5. **"WhatsApp group ok oganda"** (WhatsApp group is not community)
   - Meaning: Online groups don't replace real community
   - Usage: Bi iriwri gi ji adier, kik ibed e group kende.

---

**Modern Luo Proverbs:**

1. **Ng'at ma bedo e Facebook pile ok nyal pur lowo**
   - A person who is always on Facebook cannot farm the land
   - Meaning: Virtual activity doesn't replace real work

2. **Simu manyien ok miyo rieko manyien**
   - A new phone doesn't give new wisdom
   - Meaning: Technology doesn't replace wisdom

3. **Ng'at ma mako foto pile ok neno ng'ima adier**
   - A person who takes photos all the time doesn't see real life
   - Meaning: Don't live life through a camera lens

4. **Data teko to rieko teko moloyo**
   - Data is power but wisdom is greater power
   - Meaning: Information is good but wisdom is better

5. **Ng'at ma nigi followers mathoth to ok nigi osiep, en jachan**
   - A person with many followers but no friends is poor
   - Meaning: Real relationships matter more than online popularity
